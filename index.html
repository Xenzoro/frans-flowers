About Us

I fell in love with tulips when I was just a little girl growing up in Mexico in the late 1970s. Before I was nine, I was already planting them myself, especially the yellow ones. To me, they always felt like sunshine in flower form. When my family moved to the United States in the early 1980s, that love came with me. No matter where I lived, I always found space to grow something. Flowers have been my constant through every stage of life. After raising my children and watching them grow into their own lives, I decided to grow something else, my own floral shop here in Las Vegas, Nevada. This space is built on everything I learned early on: care deeply, stay patient, and let beauty bloom in its own time. And yes, there will always be yellow tulips nearby.
